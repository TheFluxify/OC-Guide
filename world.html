Some examples of worlds you could make

Fantasy!

Dungeons and Dragons inspired, full of mythical beasts and faraway kingdoms! This is incredibly popular and always has been in media from The Game of Thrones to House of Dragons.

Cyberpunk!

A future where everything has gone full tech, including people! Games and media like CYBERPUNK 2077 and Cyberpunk: Edgerunners feature this.

Modern!

Maybe an office! A school! An intricate city somewhere where everyone knows everyone or nobody! Most slice of life animes are set like this but there's also western medias like Two Broke Girls, The Office, Brooklyn 99.

Outerspace!

A new planet has been discovered and there's an expedition there to see if it's liveable OR from the perspective of those who might already inhabit it! Star Wars, Doctor Who and Star Trek all touch up on space.

Underwater!

Very little is known about our ocean, you could create new species! The Meg, The Swarm, The Deep and The Abyss focus on the scary unknown..

Alternative Modern!

Similar to normal modern day life but maye everyone is born with powers! Or there's a select group of people with them forming organisations like what happens in The Boys and My Hero Academia.

Reminder: Don't ever feel like you HAVE to do anything or go by conventions set by similar medias. It's always completely your choice. Make a fantasy-inspired cyberpunk world with robot knights and mecha dragons! Do what you like above all else, this is just to give some ideas!
